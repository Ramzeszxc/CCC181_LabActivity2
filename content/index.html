Why SwiftDo?

The Problem: Task Overload

In today's fast-paced world, managing tasks can be overwhelming. Standard To-Do lists often fail because they don't account for inter-task dependencies or human energy levels. Users feel stressed and unsure what to tackle next.

Core Components

Intelligent Optimization

Our core algorithm uses a unique combination of Urgency (Time Left), Effort (Time Required), and Impact (User-set Importance) to calculate a dynamic 'SwiftScore' for every task. This guarantees you are always working on the most valuable task.

Intuitive Design

The Dashboard is designed for clarity, not clutter. Critical metrics are visualized instantly, allowing users to add, categorize, and start tasks with minimal friction. Less time managing the app, more time doing the work.

Performance Insight

The comprehensive History page logs completed tasks, missed deadlines, and optimization performance over time. This data is used to fine-tune future prioritization, creating a feedback loop for continuous productivity improvement.
